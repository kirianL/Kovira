# Kovira — Product Overview

Kovira is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for building:

- Forms
- Data collection systems
- Workflows
- Automations

## Core vision

Turn simple forms into structured business processes.

## Product layers

1. Forms (input layer)
2. Submissions (data layer)
3. Workflows (logic layer)
4. Automations (execution layer)
5. Analytics (insight layer)
6. Collaboration (team layer)
